UN Belarus Express for Sustainable Development Goals will be launched on the 23rd of October, 2015. The train will visit 7 regional cities in Belarus focusing on a number of priority issues: from quality education and social inclusion, to environment and entrepreneurship and celebrate the launch of United Nations new sustainable development agenda.

EPAM will strengthen their partnership with the Universities in Belarus and is committed to the goal 4 of SGS's:

EDUCATION

We invite students and IT specialists to participate in App4Education contest. Let us think about tools for achieving the goal in Belarus and worldwide.

The contest will celebrate the launch of Sustainable Development Goals & the 70th anniversary of the United Nations. YOU will have the opportunity to showcase your APP to the UN staff, Government, Civil Society Orgs, Private sector and Embassies – It is a ONCE in a life time opportunity!!!

Key Dates

  • By Oct 09, 2015: The team submits an idea in form of completed project or prototype with a description.
  • By Oct 16, 2015: Jury selects 4 projects for the final part.
  • Oct 26: Finalists to present their prototypes in Brest. It is a good change for high visibility to honorable guests arriving to Brest with UN70Express train.

Subject

Application of any type is eligible: desktop, mobile or web. It should make the learning process easy, fun and effective.

Preference is for applications with inspiring content, visualization of scientific facts and theories. The applications can be both for children and for pupils, students and adult audiences. The main idea is to contribute to education quality, availability and removing barriers.

Applications without unique content, such as "one more" engine for training, testing system, electronic diary, etc., will have a very low chance of being selected.

Please send your applications to e-mail app4education@epam.com. The application form is free. But be sure to include your idea and (if any) a reference to a web application, screenshots or videos of the prototype, presentation or other materials, which allow jury to clearly understand and evaluate the idea and implementation proposed.
